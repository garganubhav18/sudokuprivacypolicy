This Privacy Policy explains how the Sudoku app ("the App") handles information. In short: the App works entirely offline and keeps the little data it uses on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information.

Information we handle

The App stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Player name — an optional display name you choose to type when you set a high score. You are not required to use your real name.
  • Game statistics for the leaderboard — your score, completion time, and number of mistakes for completed games, kept as a top-10 list per difficulty.

The App does not collect or process: contacts, location, device identifiers, advertising IDs, email addresses, account information, usage analytics, or any other personal data.

How information is used

The information above is used solely to provide core game functionality — namely to display your local high-score leaderboard. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Storage, transmission, and sharing

  • On-device only. Leaderboard data is saved in the App's private storage and is accessible only to the App.
  • No network transmission. The App does not request the Internet permission and does not send any data off your device. There are no servers, cloud sync, or backups operated by us.
  • No third parties. The App contains no advertising networks, analytics tools, trackers, or other third-party SDKs, so no data is shared with anyone.

Permissions

The App requests no runtime permissions and no Internet access.

Data retention and deletion

Your data stays on your device until you remove it. You can delete all data the App stores at any time by:

  • clearing the App's data in Android Settings → Apps → Sudoku → Storage → Clear data, or
  • uninstalling the App, which removes its locally stored data.

If you have enabled Android's system backup feature on your device, Android may include the App's local files in your own device backup; this is controlled by your Android settings, not by us.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the App collects no data remotely and transmits nothing off the device, no personal information about children is gathered or disclosed.

Your rights

Since all data is stored locally and never leaves your device, you remain in full control of it and can view or delete it as described above. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; as we do not collect or hold any of your data ourselves, requests are satisfied by the on-device controls above.
